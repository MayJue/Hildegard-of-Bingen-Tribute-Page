Hildegard of Bingen

A Woman of Her Time

Hildegard if Bingen was a German Benedictine abbess, writer, composer, philosopher, Christian mystic, visionary, and polymath.

In the 12th century, a woman by the name of Hildegard spoke out against the power of her time which result in her leaving a great mark in history. Hildegard was born in 1098 and was given to the church at the young age of eight. She lived her life as a nun with a deep secret that troubled her throughout her life. Hildegard was able to see vision but she told no one, hence her visions became a burden to her. Though Hildegard was timid and lack confidence in herself, she was able to become a powerful and significant figure in history through her vision that changes her life, in her 42 year of age.
